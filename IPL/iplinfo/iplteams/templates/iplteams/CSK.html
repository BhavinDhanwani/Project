The Chennai Super Kings invested a hefty sum of Rs 14 crore to secure the services of Daryl Mitchell, showcasing their commitment to bolstering their batting prowess. Additionally, Sameer Rizvi emerged as the second most expensive purchase for CSK, commanding a whopping Rs 8.40 crore. Notably, Rizvi's acquisition marks a significant milestone as the costliest uncapped Indian player in the auction.
